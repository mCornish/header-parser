Timestamp Microservice

Enter a unix timestamp or natural date as a parameter. You will recieve back a JSON object containing your timestamp in unix and natural form.

Example usage:

https://mctimestamp.herokuapp.com/1450137600

OR

https://mctimestamp.herokuapp.com/December%2015,%202015

Example output:

{"unix": 1450137600, "natural": "December 15, 2015"}
